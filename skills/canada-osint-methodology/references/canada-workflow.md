# Canada Workflow

## Source families

- Government of Canada
- Provincial and municipal sources
- Canada411 and similar directories
- CanLII and legal references
- Open Canada data
- Business and securities registries

## Investigation order

1. Validate Canadian context
2. Choose the best regional source family
3. Run Canada-specific pivots
4. Record the province or municipality when known
5. Separate confirmed public records from search leads

## Reporting reminders

- Label the source family for each important result
- Keep regional pivots visible in the next-step section
- Flag uncertainty when the Canadian linkage is inferred rather than confirmed
