# Canada Localization

## Goal

Support investigations that need stronger Canada-oriented research paths, especially for geolocation, public records, companies, and regional search workflows.

## Source Hubs

- Canada Start.me hub: [OSINT Resources in Canada](https://start.me/p/aLe0vp/osint-resources-in-canada)
- Global Start.me hub: [OSINT4ALL](https://start.me/p/L1rEYQ/osint4all)

## Project Use

The Canada profile should prioritize:

- Canadian business lookup pivots
- Canada-friendly search engines and map workflows
- province and municipality record pivots
- local media and public notices
- location-first person and company research

## Practical Modeling

Inside this repository, Canada localization should be handled as:

- a profile in `config/profiles/canada_localization.yaml`
- report tagging such as `canada`, `province`, `municipality`, `geo`
- additional pivot URLs for company and person-name research

## Important Note

The Start.me pages were accessible as pages, but their full structured bookmark contents were not machine-readable in this environment. They are therefore included here as curated external hubs to review and expand manually inside the registry over time.
